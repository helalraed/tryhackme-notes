🔵 Blue Team Terms
.Blue Team: Defenders who protect systems and respond to threats
.Client Infrastructure: Systems, servers, and devices that need protection
.Visibility: Ability to monitor system activity
.Threat: Potential danger (attacker, malware, etc.)
.Prevention: Stopping attacks before they happen
.Detection: Identifying suspicious activity
.Mitigation: Reducing impact after an attack
.Risk: Likelihood and impact of a threat
