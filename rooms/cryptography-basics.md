## 🎯 Objective

---

## 🧠 What I Learned

* Difference between encryption and decryption
* Symmetric vs asymmetric encryption
* Caesar Cipher and why it is insecure
* Basics of XOR and modulo operations

---

## 🔍 Key Notes

* Caesar Cipher is weak (only 25 possible keys)
* Symmetric encryption is fast but has key distribution issues
* Asymmetric encryption solves the key exchange problem
* XOR can be used for simple encryption
* Modulo is widely used in cryptographic algorithms

---

## 💡 Takeaways

* Cryptography is essential for cybersecurity
* Understanding concepts is more important than memorizing
* Security depends heavily on protecting keys
